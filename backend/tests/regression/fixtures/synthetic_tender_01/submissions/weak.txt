Bramblefield Contractors Limited — Grounds Maintenance Tender

Question Q1
Bramblefield Contractors has many years of experience in grounds maintenance and we are confident we can mobilise this contract quickly. Our team is flexible and used to picking up new work at short notice. We would aim to have everything in place for the start date and would discuss the details of schedules with the council once the contract is awarded. We always do our best to keep services running whatever the weather.

Question Q2.1
We have a pool of experienced staff and take on seasonal workers in the summer as needed. Our staff are trained on the job by our longer-serving employees and we make sure everyone can use the equipment safely before they start. Spraying work would be subcontracted to a specialist if required.

Question Q2.2
Our owner visits the teams regularly to check on progress and deal with any issues. Staff facilities are available at our yard. We look after our people and most of our staff have been with us for years.

Question Q3.1
We pride ourselves on the quality of our work and our customers are generally very satisfied. If the council reports a problem we will come back and put it right as soon as we can. We keep records of the work we have completed in our diary system.

Question Q3.2
We are happy to meet with the council as often as needed to discuss the contract. We would provide updates on work completed when requested. Our approach is to keep things simple and sort issues out quickly by phone.

Question Q4
We comply with all environmental regulations and dispose of green waste responsibly at a licensed facility. We use pesticides carefully and only when needed. We would consider electric equipment in future if prices come down, as we believe in doing our bit for the environment.

Question Q5
We are a local firm and most of our staff live in the district. Supporting the local community is important to us.

Question Q6
We take health and safety very seriously and have a health and safety policy which is reviewed annually. Staff are reminded to take care when working near roads and we provide high-visibility clothing and cones. Any accidents would be reported to the relevant authorities as required by law.
